The Beginning of the Universe

The Big Bang was not an explosion; it was silent, with no light or sound. Time and space began at the Big Bang—there was no “before.” The narrative we’re told is simplified; the true origin may be beyond comprehension..

Physical constants are so precisely balanced that slight changes would make life impossible.

Raises the possibility of intelligent design or a multiverse where ours is one of the rare, life-supporting versions.
